Our Unique 5-Step Process to Better Health

Our approach is systematic and thorough, designed to uncover and address the root causes of your health challenges:

1 In-Depth Consultation

Unlike rushed medical visits, we take the time to truly understand your complete health journey. For children, this includes pregnancy and birth history, early developmental milestones, and every factor that may have contributed to current challenges.

This comprehensive history helps us identify potential root causes that other providers might miss. We're looking for the "Perfect Storm" of factors—birth trauma, early illnesses, toxins, stress—that may have led to nervous system dysfunction.

  • Complete health history from pregnancy through present
  • Previous treatments and what has or hasn't worked
  • Current symptoms and daily challenges
  • Your goals and hopes for care

2 Advanced INSiGHT Scans (COREscore)

We utilize state-of-the-art technology to measure your nervous system function. These scans detect three crucial factors that other providers often miss:

What Our Scans Measure:

  • Subluxation Patterns: Areas of spinal misalignment creating nerve interference
  • Dysautonomia: Imbalance between "fight or flight" and "rest and digest" states
  • Vagus Nerve Dysfunction: Affecting digestion, heart rate, emotional regulation, immune function
  • Surface EMG: Measuring muscle tension patterns along the spine
  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV): Your body's ability to adapt to stress
  • Thermal Scanning: Detecting areas of autonomic imbalance

This gives us objective data about your neurological health—taking the guesswork out of care. The scans are completely non-invasive, painless, and take about 10-15 minutes.

3 Customized Care Plan Development

We blend your unique history with your scan results to create a precisely tailored care plan. There are no "cookie-cutter" approaches here—every aspect of care is specifically calculated for optimal results:

  • Specific adjustment frequency based on your severity and chronicity
  • Estimated timeline with realistic expectations
  • Milestone markers to track your progress
  • Re-scan schedule to measure objective improvement
  • Investment options and family plans

4 Comprehensive Report of Findings

We take time to explain everything we've discovered:

  • Walk through your scan results with clear, visual explanations
  • Connect your health history to what we see in the nervous system
  • Explain exactly what we found and what it means for you
  • Answer all your questions thoroughly
  • Outline clear next steps

You'll leave understanding your (or your child's) nervous system better than ever before—and with a clear path forward.

5 Gentle, Effective Adjustments

Our adjustments are safe, gentle, and specifically designed for all ages—from newborns to seniors:

  • Talsky Tonal Chiropractic (TTC): Working with meningeal system tension for deep neurological impact
  • ArthroStim Instrument: Precise, comfortable corrections using gentle instrumentation
  • No cracking, popping, or twisting: So gentle that babies often sleep through adjustments
  • Position-specific adjusting: We can adjust you in the exact posture that causes your symptoms

Each adjustment helps release tension and stress from the nervous system, often leading to improved sleep, behavior, digestion, focus, and overall well-being.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I get adjusted on my first visit?

Typically, no. We believe in doing a thorough evaluation first and taking time to explain our findings before beginning care. Your first adjustment usually happens at your second visit (Report of Findings) after you fully understand what we've found.

Is it safe for my baby/child?

Absolutely. Our techniques are so gentle that babies often sleep through their adjustments. We've cared for newborns just days old and have extensive experience with children of all ages and abilities—including those with sensory sensitivities.

What if I'm nervous about chiropractic?

Many of our patients felt the same way! Our gentle, instrument-based techniques are nothing like traditional "cracking" adjustments. There's no twisting, popping, or sudden movements. Most people find our care relaxing and comfortable.

How long until I see results?

This varies based on your specific situation. Some people notice changes immediately; others take several weeks. We'll give you realistic expectations based on your scan results and health history, and we track progress with periodic re-scans so you can see objective improvement.

Do I need a referral?

No referral is needed. You can come directly to us.
